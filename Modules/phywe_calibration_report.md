## 4. Operational Notes and Recommendations
- The apparatus passed all mechanical, electrical, and vacuum tests.  
- Reported values (voltages, currents, thermocouple readings) serve **for orientation only**; perform a **user calibration** before quantitative measurements.  
- Maintain vacuum better than 5 × 10⁻⁶ mbar during operation.  
- **Demagnetize** the magnet after each run to remove residual field.  
- The 70 mm × 2.5 mm pole geometry corresponds to standard PHYWE SG-100 magnetic-field gradients (~10 T m⁻¹ order).  
- Allow ~10 minutes of oven warm-up for thermal equilibrium.
